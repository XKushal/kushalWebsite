This web application was built at HACKUIOWA and received the Best Market Potential Award. It was designed to make student election voting easier to access and easier to manage.

## What I Built

The project used a lightweight Python and Flask stack to support a focused voting workflow for student elections, with an emphasis on clarity and practical usefulness.
